Start Assesment

Use this assessment to see if you need to contact a mental health professional for depression diagnosis and treatment, or to keep track of your mood.



Instructions:

You can make a copy of this scale and use it to track your moods on a weekly basis. It could also be utilised to demonstrate to your doctor how your symptoms have altered over time. Changes of five points or more are considered noteworthy. This scale is not intended to be used to make a depression diagnosis or to replace a professional diagnosis. If you think you might be depressed, seek help from a mental health professional as soon as possible.
